About Agadir

Agadir is a vibrant coastal city on Morocco’s southern Atlantic coast, famous for its golden beaches, modern marina, and the historic Kasbah overlooking the bay. Known for year-round sunshine and a relaxed beachfront atmosphere, Agadir offers a perfect blend of cultural heritage, outdoor adventure, and world-class amenities. Whether you’re looking to surf the waves in Taghazout, explore the lush Paradise Valley, or savor fresh seafood at the marina, Agadir has something for every traveler.
